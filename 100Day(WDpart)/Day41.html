Top 3 Islamic Contemperay works according to Youssef Amr


My top 3 of all time

1. Al-Muwatta" by Imam Malik

One of the earliest collections of Hadith and Islamic legal traditions, "Al-Muwatta" is an important text for understanding Islamic law (Fiqh) according to the Maliki school of thought.

2. "Al-Risala" by Imam al-Shafi'i

This foundational text outlines the principles of Islamic legal theory, including the interpretation of the Quran and Hadith, and the methodologies for deriving legal rulings.

3. Musnad Ahmad ibn Hanbal

Musnad Ahmad ibn Hanbal" is one of the largest collections of Hadith in Islamic literature, compiled by the famous Islamic scholar and theologian, Ahmad ibn Hanbal. The collection includes over 27,000 Hadiths, organized according to the companions of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) who narrated them. This work is highly respected in the Islamic world and is used as a reference for understanding the sayings and practices of the Prophet Muhammad.
